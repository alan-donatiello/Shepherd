Help

1. Connect a wallet
Go to Connections and paste any Base (0x...) or Solana wallet address. Set the lookback window and click Scan. Shepherd reads USDC transfer events directly from the blockchain.
2. Classify transactions
In the Ledger, step through unclassified transactions and assign each to a GL code. When you classify one counterparty, all other transactions from that address auto-classify.
3. Review financial statements
As you classify, the Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement build in real time from on-chain data. When all transactions are classified, you get a fully reconciled close.
Supported chains
Base (EVM) and Solana mainnet. USDC transfers only. Ethereum mainnet and additional stablecoins coming soon.
